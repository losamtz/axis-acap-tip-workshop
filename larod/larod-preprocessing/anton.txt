I) Model compilation

- Offline compilation
    - Model is compiled to natibe format on packages
    - Requires vendor toolchains
    - More customization 
- Online compilation
    - model is compiled in the camera
- Compilation time
    - DLPU is slow
    - GPU is fast
    - CPU almost instant

J) Model Caching

When a .tflite model is loaded we cache the compiled  subgraph (operation from dlpu)
Instant model loading after the first time
Saved to filesytem, cache is persistent

K) Debugging and profiling

- No standard for debugging/profiling
- Vendor support is often limited
- Disallow from distributing to partners
- We are still looking at ways to get more profiling information out of tflite
